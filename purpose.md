# Purpose

This repo is a reusable LLM-maintained business-idea wiki. Its job is to turn raw notes, interviews, and research into a compact evidence-backed view of which ideas are worth pursuing, parking, or killing.

## Decision Standard

An idea becomes stronger when the wiki can name:

- A specific customer with budget authority.
- A painful, frequent, or expensive problem.
- Evidence that the customer will pay or change behavior.
- A wedge that can be tested before overbuilding.
- Risks that are concrete enough to disconfirm.

An idea stays weak when the case rests mainly on vibes, friend feedback, broad TAM, vague AI leverage, or unsupported founder belief.

## Current Portfolio

No idea content is loaded by default. In Notion-backed mode, add idea-specific raw material under Notion `raw/<idea-slug>/`, cross-idea material under Notion `raw/shared/`, then ingest it into Notion `wiki/<idea-slug>/`.

## Current Gating Questions

- What is the next raw source to ingest?
- Which idea, if any, should become the first active case in this wiki?
- What evidence would change the verdict fastest?
